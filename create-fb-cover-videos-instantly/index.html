Hassles in creating videos for Facebook Cover

Facebook cover video is the custard to your pie. However, you should be mindful of the complexities that come along with using a regular video for your Facebook page cover. Here are some instances:

  • The video resolution needs to be an exact 820 X 312 px
  • The video length should be at least 20 and at most of 90 seconds
  • The preferred video quality is 1080p
  • The content must be present on the center of the frame so that you have least probability of losing it if needed to be cropped.

“With great opportunities comes great responsibilities”. This saying fits right when it comes to using a video to your FB cover slot. It requires you to be mindful of the complexities.
