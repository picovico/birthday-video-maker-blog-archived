Picovico is a marvelous platform for you to create loads of different kinds videos. And by different kinds, we mean, you can explore your creativity and just make anything you want. This simple tool lets you seamlessly convert photos into videos without so much as having to open any complicated software.

You just upload your photos, put captions, choose some music, and that’s it, you’re done. Your video is made.

Now, we understand that everyone wants to be a youtuber these days but not everyone has the gadgets (camera, mic, and so forth) or the skills required to be a proficient film-maker. To be a successful film-maker or video maker, like youtubers such as Pewdiepie, Jacksepticeye, Ryan Nigahiga, you will need loads of different things rather than just will to do it. Your sheer determination isn’t enough either.

You will need special set of skills that involves video editing, camera work, and so much more. That’s right, if it was that easy everyone would be a millionaire off youtube.

Tell you what though, it’s not entirely impossible for us normal people to hit the limelight of youtube either. Now, there are plenty of different varieties of youtube videos that ranges from vlogs to vines, and even slideshows. With the use of our tool, Picovico, you could very well be a youtuber with thousands of subscribers.

What’s the First Step?

The first step is to head on to picovico.com and sign up. Once you’re registered, you are probably better off signing up for our Pro account. The reason for that is if you’re not signed up for Pro account, while you can still make videos you’ll get a watermark at the bottom right of the video that says “Made with Picovico”.

So, if you want to establish yourself as a youtuber, it’s a very bad idea to have someone else’s brand on your video. With the Pro, your video is watermark free. There’s also a CTA button at the end of the video with the Pro account, where you can call out your audience to like or subscribe the video and it will be a brilliant way to interact with your audience.

What sort of Youtube videos can I create?

Now let’s get to the real question. There is plenty you can do, really, but the most important thing is to figure out what you want to do. The possibilities are endless, and you need to figure out the idea you’re interested in. Do you want to create an autobiography of your favorite celebrities? Do you want to create short photo videos Buzzfeed used to make?

Well, if you’re completely lost and just want to create videos and be a successful youtuber (hopefully), you’re in the right place. Here are some ideas out the top of my head.

Biographies

If you’ve ever watched TMZ, they used to do this thing where they showed life stories through several different pictures. Actually, I’m not sure if it was TMZ but you know what I am talking about. Say, you want to create a biography video of George Clooney. All you need to do is gather a bunch of photos of George from when he was a kid. You could add important incidents in his life and so forth. And you can do all this in Picovico by simply clicking on “Next”.

Top 10 Charts

I probably don’t even have to explain this one. You see plenty of these on the internet every single day. You could do a Top 10 for virtually anything. And with photos, it’s so simple yet so effective because people easily understand what you mean, and you don’t need to put in terrible amount of effort either. Just make sure the top 10 chart you create can grab people’s interest. Some examples would be top 10 vegan restaurants, top 10 musicians of 2016 and so forth. Remember, these things tend to vary depending on the demographic you’re targeting, so you’ll need to do your homework.

Hottest News

If you plan to create a video every week, then this is another idea you can look into. You could just gather the news highlights of the week and feature them in your video. Again, it’s super easy. All you need to do is gather images that are relevant to the news, put in some captions and you’re done.

Meme Collection

Internet is filled with memes and there are definitely people all round the world who want to look at memes. If you have the time and patience to search and create memes, then this idea could be for you. Since picovico takes care of the video rendering after you’ve processed the images, this could be one of the greatest video idea in the world of slideshow video-making.

At last,

I have thrown in some pretty amazing ideas in the lot here. Now, I am not saying your videos will necessarily go viral and make you a successful youtuber overnight. This is just some of the things to get started. Maybe you will be motivated to buy the gadgets you need to create better videos in the future by getting started here.

Good luck on your journey.
